Sausage and Potatoes Bake

Description

This recipe is so good.

Really, it is so very good.

Ingredients
  • Fully cooked Sasuage
  • Whole Potatoes
  • Various Seasonings
Steps
  1. Chop Potatoes into 1 inch cubes
  2. Place chopped Potatoes onto tinfoil sheet on baking sheet
  3. Cook for 15min at 400F
  4. After 15min, place sasuage on baking sheet. Ensure proper spacing. Cook for another 15min
