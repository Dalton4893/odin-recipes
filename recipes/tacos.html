Tacos

Description

These Tacos are so good.

Really, they is so very good.

Ingredients
  • Flour or Corn Tortillas
  • Taco Seasoning
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Chedder Cheese
  • Various Greens
Steps
  1. Cook Ground Beef until fully brown
  2. Place cooked Ground Beef on the Tortilla
  3. Place cheese on the Ground Beef
  4. Place your various greens on the cheese
