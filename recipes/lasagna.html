Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna recipe is so good.

Really, it is so very good.

Ingredients
  • No-Cook Lasagna Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Chedder Cheese
  • Parmensan Cheese
Steps
  1. Cook Ground Beef until fully brown
  2. Mix cooked Ground Beef with Chedder and Parmensan Cheese
  3. Mix half jar of Marinara Sauce with Beef and Cheese mix
  4. Spread Marinara Sauce across bottom of baking dish
  5. Lay first layer of no-cook Lasagna Pasta
  6. Spread Beef and Cheese mix along the layer of Lasagna Pasta
  7. Repeat steps 5 & 6 until you have placed a total 4 layers of Lasagna Pasta
  8. Place in oven at 350F with tinfoil cover for 30min
